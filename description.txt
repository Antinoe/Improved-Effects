A simple mod about adding more visual and audible detail to Player Movement.

As an example, there are now Step sounds. They are entirely dependent on what the Player is currently wearing. Silver Armor sounds heavier than Wood Armor, for example. And if no footwear is equipped, then Bare Feet sounds will play instead.

__Brief Features__
Sounds:
-Stepping
-Jumping
-Landing
-Various Armor Rustles
-Various Footwear Steps

Screenshake:
-Falling
-Landing
